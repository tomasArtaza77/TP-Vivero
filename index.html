high quality since 1998

'Nombre del vivero' was founded in 1998 in Los Polvorines, Buenos Aires and has since grown to become one of the largest bedding plant producers in Argentina. Our five growing locations gives us the capability to provide both the quality and quantity of annual color needed by landscape contractors to beautify both residential and commercial green spaces all year long. Additionally, our two sales locations and delivery fleet make pick-up and delivery fast and efficient across the country.
